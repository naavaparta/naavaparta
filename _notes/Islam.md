Islam, in romanized Arabic *al-islam*, "submission (to God)", is a monotheistic religion. The holy book of Islam is [[The Qur'an]]. Approximately 2 billion people, 25 percent of the world's population, follow Islam.

Branches:
- [[Sunni]]
	- [[Salafism]]
- [[Shia]]
